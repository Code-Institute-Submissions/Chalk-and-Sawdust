Chalk and Sawdust PL Gym

ABOUT

Chalk and Sawdust Powerlifting Gym is training facility focusing on the principles of powerlifting to help people of all abilities and age ranges improve their strength and fitness. Powerlifting is an individual sport combining 3 disciplines; the squat, the bench press and the deadlift. Chalk and Sawdust Powerlifting Gym provides the equipment, the environment and the knowledge required to excel in these disciplines and become the best lifter you can be.
