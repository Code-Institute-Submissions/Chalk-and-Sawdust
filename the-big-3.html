The Big 3

Here at Chalk and Sawdust powerlifting gym we cannot stress the importance of "the big 3" enough; the big 3 being the squat, the bench press and the deadlift. These 3 lifts should be the staple of any strength training regime. It is equally as important for high level athletes as it is for the average person trying gain strength or for older people looking to regain control over their body

The Squat

The squat helps build strength and power in the legs and posterior chain.

The Bench press

The bench press is a great tool for building upper-body strength and mass, particularly the chest and triceps.

The Deadlift

The deadlift is almost universally considered the number one exercise for overall physical strength.
